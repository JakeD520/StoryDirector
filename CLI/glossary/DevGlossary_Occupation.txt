STRUCTURE: QUIRKS, OBSESSIONS, HABITS
Category	Description
Quirks	Odd or distinctive behaviors — talks to plants, avoids eye contact, whistles when lying
Obsession / Vice	Fixations — gambling, collecting antique maps, body modifications
Social Style	Communication vibe — overly blunt, charming, awkward, overly formal
Habits	Repetitive patterns — sharpens blade obsessively, hums sea shanties, taps teeth
Fears / Avoidance	Unspoken phobias or discomforts — hates confined spaces, avoids mirrors
